## Current State

### Soil
- **Type:** Sandy, low organic matter
- **Structure:** Poor, easily eroded
- **Infiltration:** Low, high runoff
- **Biology:** Minimal

### Vegetation
- **Cover:** <10% degraded grasses
- **Species:** Remnant Stipa, drought-stressed
- **Trend:** Declining

### Current Efforts
- **Three-North Shelterbelt** (Great Green Wall) nearby
- Some tree plantations with mixed success
- Government conservation programs
- Research stations in region

### Key Challenges
1. Extreme temperature range
2. High evaporation
3. Strong winds
4. Sandy soil erosion
5. Limited water sources

## Gap Analysis

### What's Being Done
- Large-scale tree planting
- Sand dune stabilization
- Research programs

### What's Working
- Straw checkerboards for dune stabilization
- Some shelterbelt success
- Government commitment

### What's Not Working
- Tree survival without irrigation
- Water scarcity limiting expansion
- Sandy soil drainage issues

### What's Missing
- Integrated water capture + storage
- Subsurface irrigation systems
- Biological soil activation
- Community-scale interventions

### Our Opportunity
- Combine Zaï/half-moons with underground ollas
- Leverage existing shelterbelt infrastructure
- Focus on soil biology activation
- Design for extreme temperature range

## Technical Specifications

### Zone A: Wind Protection
- **Technique:** Straw checkerboards
- **Size:** 1x1 m grids, 20 cm height
- **Material:** Wheat/rice straw
- **Purpose:** Reduce wind erosion, trap sand

### Zone B: Water Capture
- **Zaï pits:**
  - Diameter: 40 cm
  - Depth: 20 cm
  - Spacing: 80 cm grid
  - Fill: Compost + biochar (70/30)

- **Half-moons:**
  - Diameter: 3 m
  - Depth: 25 cm
  - Spacing: 8 m apart
  - Orientation: Facing upslope

### Zone C: Water Storage/Delivery
- **Ollas:**
  - Size: 15-20 L capacity
  - Burial: 35 cm depth
  - Spacing: 2 m apart
  - Connection: Gravel-filled trenches from Zone B

- **Gravity channels:**
  - Width: 20 cm
  - Depth: 15 cm
  - Slope: 2%
  - Lining: Gravel/sand

### Zone D: Vegetation
- **Grasses (Year 1):**
  - Stipa capillata
  - Agropyron cristatum
  - Spacing: 30 cm

- **Shrubs (Year 1-2):**
  - Caragana korshinskii (nitrogen fixer)
  - Hedysarum scoparium
  - Spacing: 2 m

- **Trees (Year 2-3):**
  - Elaeagnus angustifolia
  - Populus euphratica (in wetter zones)
  - Spacing: 5 m

## Risks & Mitigations

| Risk | Likelihood | Mitigation |
|------|------------|------------|
| Extreme cold damages ollas | Medium | Use frost-resistant pots, deep burial |
| Drought year | High | Emergency water backup plan |
| Wind damage | Medium | Prioritize straw grid installation |
| Pest damage | Low | Use native, resistant species |
| Access issues | Low | Partner with local research station |
