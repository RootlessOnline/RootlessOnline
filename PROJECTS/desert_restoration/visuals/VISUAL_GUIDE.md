## How It All Works Together

```
1. RAIN FALLS
   ↓
2. ZAÏ PITS CAPTURE WATER
   - Breaks hard soil crust
   - Concentrates water in one spot
   - Organic matter holds moisture
   ↓
3. GRAVITY CHANNELS MOVE WATER
   - Slight slope (1-3%)
   - Underground trenches
   - Gravel/sand fill
   ↓
4. OLLAS STORE WATER UNDERGROUND
   - Below evaporation zone
   - Porous walls release slowly
   - Self-regulating (soil moisture)
   ↓
5. PLANTS ACCESS WATER
   - Roots grow toward moisture
   - Consistent supply
   - No surface evaporation
   ↓
6. SOIL BIOLOGY ACTIVATES
   - Organic matter feeds microbes
   - Termites create channels
   - Soil structure improves
   ↓
7. VEGETATION ESTABLISHES
   - Higher survival rates
   - Faster growth
   - Self-sustaining over time
```
