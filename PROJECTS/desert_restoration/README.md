## Vision

Create a scalable framework for reversing desertification in multiple locations worldwide by combining the most effective soil revival techniques:

1. **Water Capture** — Zaï pits, half-moon basins, swales
2. **Subsurface Irrigation** — Underground ollas fed by gravity
3. **Biological Regeneration** — Pioneer plants, soil fauna, fungi
4. **Soil Enhancement** — Biochar, compost, organic matter
5. **Community Engagement** — Local ownership and stewardship

---

## The Core Innovation

**Traditional approaches fail because:**
- Trees die without water support
- Surface water evaporates quickly
- Soil biology is ignored
- Projects are top-down, not community-led

**Our integrated approach:**
- Captures rainwater in pits
- Stores water underground in ollas (minimal evaporation)
- Feeds soil biology with organic matter
- Builds from the ground up (literally)

## Location Strategy

### Phase 1: Research Locations

For each potential location, we need:
1. **Desertification status** — How fast is it expanding?
2. **Current efforts** — Who's already working there?
3. **Gaps** — What's not being done?
4. **Feasibility** — Can we contribute?
5. **Partnerships** — Who could we work with?

### Phase 2: Prioritize

Rank locations by:
- Impact potential (speed of soil recovery)
- Partnership opportunities
- Resource requirements
- Community readiness
- Political stability

### Phase 3: Design & Implement

Create location-specific plans combining:
- Water harvesting structures
- Subsurface irrigation networks
- Vegetation strategy
- Community engagement model
- Monitoring system
