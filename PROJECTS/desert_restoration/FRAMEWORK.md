# Desert Restoration — Location Framework

> How to evaluate, prioritize, and plan desert restoration locations.

---

## Overview

This framework provides a systematic approach to:
1. Identify locations facing desertification
2. Research current efforts and gaps
3. Evaluate feasibility and potential impact
4. Design location-specific interventions
5. Monitor and adapt over time

## Part 4: Intervention Design

### Technique Selection Guide

| Condition | Recommended Techniques |
|-----------|------------------------|
| Low rainfall (<200mm) | Zaï pits + ollas + mulch |
| Sandy soil | Straw checkerboards + deep ollas |
| Clay soil | Half-moons + swales |
| Some vegetation | FMNR + supplement |
| No vegetation | Full intervention needed |
| High evaporation | Subsurface ollas essential |

### Design Process

1. **Map the site**
   - Contours and slopes
   - Existing features
   - Water flow patterns

2. **Design water capture**
   - Place Zaï pits on contours
   - Design half-moons for runoff areas
   - Plan swales for larger flows

3. **Design water storage/delivery**
   - Place ollas at root zones
   - Create gravity channels from pits
   - Ensure proper depth and spacing

4. **Plan vegetation**
   - Select pioneer species
   - Plan succession stages
   - Include nitrogen fixers

5. **Plan soil enhancement**
   - Compost/manure for pits
   - Biochar application
   - Mulch coverage

6. **Plan community engagement**
   - Identify stakeholders
   - Design training program
   - Plan ongoing support

## Part 5: Monitoring Framework

### What to Monitor

| Category | Indicators | Method |
|----------|------------|--------|
| Water | Soil moisture, water captured | Sensors, observation |
| Soil | Organic matter, structure | Lab tests, observation |
| Vegetation | Cover %, species count | Satellite, ground survey |
| Biology | Microbes, fauna activity | Lab tests, observation |
| Community | Participation, adoption | Surveys, records |

### Timeline for Monitoring

| Time | What to Check |
|------|---------------|
| Weekly | Olla water levels, plant survival |
| Monthly | Soil moisture, pit condition |
| Quarterly | Vegetation growth, erosion |
| Annually | Soil organic matter, biodiversity |
| 5-year | Ecosystem establishment |

### Success Indicators

| Time | Target |
|------|--------|
| 3 months | Water capture working, initial plant survival |
| 6 months | Soil biology active, vegetation establishing |
| 1 year | Measurable soil improvement, plant growth |
| 3 years | Self-sustaining vegetation, community ownership |
| 5 years | Ecosystem functioning, desertification reversed locally |
