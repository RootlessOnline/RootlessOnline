## Vision

A political movement that brings regenerative, community-first politics to the Netherlands — starting from the ground up, not top-down.

**Not just another party.** A new way of doing politics:
- Regenerative policies (leave things better)
- Community ownership (power from below)
- Practical action (not just talk)
- Transparent process (everything documented)

---

## Core Approach

### Chaordic Politics

Following our organizational model:
- **Purpose** → Clear vision of regenerative communities
- **Principles** → Transparent, inclusive, regenerative
- **People** → Community-led, not politician-led
- **Concepts** → Participatory democracy, citizen assemblies
- **Structure** → Decentralized, municipal focus
- **Practice** → Start local, build up
