## Key Components

### 1. Olla Irrigation Systems

**What:** Clay pot irrigation that saves 60-70% water.

**Why:**
- Low maintenance
- Water-efficient
- Works during vacations
- Easy to make or buy

**Research:** University of Arizona studies show significant water savings.

**Next Steps:**
- Research suppliers
- Test in first garden
- Document installation process

### 2. Permaculture Design

**What:** Design system that works with nature.

**Benefits:**
- 27% higher soil carbon
- 201% more earthworms
- Self-sustaining after establishment
- Low input requirements

**Next Steps:**
- Learn permaculture principles
- Design for first site
- Document for replication

### 3. Therapeutic Horticulture

**What:** Gardening as therapy and integration support.

**Connection:** MAPS recommends horticultural therapy for psychedelic integration.

**Opportunity:** Partner with retreat centers like Primal Nature (Eric Haas).

**Next Steps:**
- Research therapeutic horticulture protocols
- Prepare pitch for Eric Haas
- Design garden with therapy in mind
