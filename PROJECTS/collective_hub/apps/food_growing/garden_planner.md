# Garden Planner

> Design, plan, and manage your garden.

| Property | Value |

---

## Core Purpose

Help people design, plan, and maintain gardens more easily, especially beginners.

**Problems it solves:**
- Gardening knowledge is fragmented
- Planning crop rotations is difficult
- People forget planting and harvest times

---

## Key Features

### Visual Layout Planner
- Drag-and-drop garden beds
- Plant placement
- Spacing guides
- Multiple bed layouts

### Planting Calendar
- When to plant each crop
- Frost dates
- Seasonal planning
- Reminder notifications

### Crop Rotation
- Track what was planted where
- Rotation recommendations
- Soil health tracking

### Harvest Tracking
- What's ready to harvest
- Yield logging
- Season summary

### Smart Suggestions
- Companion planting
- Sun requirements
- Water needs
- Soil type matching

### Task Management
- Daily/weekly tasks
- Watering schedule
- Fertilizing reminders
- Pest monitoring

---

## User Flow

```
1. Create garden → Enter size, location, climate
2. Design layout → Drag-and-drop beds and plants
3. Get recommendations → Planting times, companions
4. Follow plan → Reminders for tasks
5. Track progress → Harvest logs, photos
```
