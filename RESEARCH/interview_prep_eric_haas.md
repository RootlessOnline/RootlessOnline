### "We already have integration support"

*That's great. I'm not proposing to replace what works. I'm offering something different — an ongoing, nature-based practice that participants can access between sessions. Many people need that regular grounding."*

### "How would this actually work logistically?"

*Great question. I'm flexible and want to design this with you. Options could include:*
- *Garden visits as part of retreat schedule*
- *Ongoing garden access for past participants*
- *Referral to therapeutic gardening sessions*
- *Co-branded "integration garden" program*

### "What are your qualifications?"

*I'm transparent: I'm building this from the ground up. What I bring:*
- *Deep research into therapeutic horticulture*
- *Commitment to doing this right*
- *Willingness to learn from practitioners like you*
- *A network of AI support for documentation and best practices*

### "What's the cost?"

*For a pilot, I'm open to creative arrangements. My goal is to prove the concept and build something valuable. If participants benefit, we can discuss sustainable models together."*
