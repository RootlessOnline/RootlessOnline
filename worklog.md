**Source: Yale Sustainable Food Program**
- Finding: "Olla irrigation relies on porosity of low-fired clay to deliver steady water supply right to roots"
- Mechanism confirmed: Porous clay + soil moisture differential

### How It Works (Scientific Mechanism)

1. **Porous Clay Structure**: Low-fired clay has microscopic pores
2. **Pressure Differential**: When soil is dry → water pressure inside olla is higher → water seeps out
3. **Equilibrium State**: When soil is wet → pressure equalizes → water stays inside
4. **Capillary Action**: Water moves through soil via capillary forces to plant roots
5. **Self-Regulating**: No timers, sensors, or electricity needed

### Harley's Innovation

**Traditional Olla**: Manual refill required
**Harley's System**: 
- Rainwater barrel (raised for gravity flow)
- Float valve (vlotter) for automatic refill
- Tubing connection
- Zero electricity
- Fully automated once installed

### Comparison: Olla vs Drip Irrigation

| Feature | Olla Irrigation | Drip Irrigation |
|---------|-----------------|-----------------|
| Water Savings | 60-70% | 30-50% |
| Electricity | ZERO | Pumps/timers needed |
| Maintenance | Low (refill/check valve) | Medium (clogs, repairs) |
| Cost | Low (clay pots) | Medium-High |
| Root Growth | Encourages deep roots | Shallow root risk |
| Automation | Passive (physics) | Active (electronics) |
| Lifespan | Years (clay durable) | Years (plastic degrades) |
