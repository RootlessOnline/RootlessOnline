## Chat 1: Deep Research — Global Desertification Status

**Focus:** Where is desertification happening? What's being done? What's missing?

### Questions for ChatGPT:

1. **Major Desertification Hotspots**
   - Top 10 regions with fastest desert expansion
   - Rate of expansion (km²/year)
   - Primary causes (climate, human activity, etc.)

2. **Current Global Efforts**
   - Major organizations working on desert restoration
   - Government programs by country
   - NGO initiatives
   - Research institutions

3. **Gaps Analysis**
   - Where is nothing being done?
   - Where are current efforts failing?
   - What techniques are underutilized?
   - What funding gaps exist?

**Output Needed:** Summary report with data table of regions

## Chat 2: Technical Deep Dive — Zaï + Olla Integration

**Focus:** Engineering details for the gravity-fed olla network

### Questions for ChatGPT:

1. **Hydrology Engineering**
   - Optimal slope percentages for gravity flow
   - Channel materials and construction
   - Water flow rate calculations
   - Soil moisture distribution patterns

2. **Olla Specifications**
   - Best clay compositions for different soils
   - Optimal wall thickness for release rate
   - Size-to-plant ratios by species
   - Frost resistance for cold deserts (Gobi)

3. **System Integration**
   - Zaï pit to olla connection designs
   - Overflow management
   - Scaling factors (garden → hectare → km²)

**Output Needed:** Technical specifications with calculations
