## Scenarios

### Scenario 1: Temporary Absence (< 7 days)

**Situation:** Harley unavailable for less than a week (vacation, illness, etc.)

**Protocol:**
1. Z continues routine operations
2. Non-urgent decisions queued in current_state.md
3. Urgent decisions follow Level 3 escalation
4. Z documents all actions taken
5. Harley reviews upon return

**Authority During Absence:**
- Z can make routine decisions
- Z can apply precedent
- Z cannot make major decisions
- Z cannot modify CONSTITUTION.md or BYLAWS.md

### Scenario 2: Extended Absence (7-30 days)

**Situation:** Harley unavailable for extended period

**Protocol:**
1. Activate successor (see succession chain below)
2. Successor assumes CEO authority temporarily
3. Z coordinates with successor
4. All decisions flagged as "temporary CEO authority"
5. Harley reviews upon return, can reverse decisions

**Authority:**
- Successor has full CEO authority temporarily
- Cannot modify CONSTITUTION.md
- Decisions reversible by Harley upon return

### Scenario 3: Long-Term Unavailability (> 30 days)

**Situation:** Harley unavailable for extended period without clear return date

**Protocol:**
1. Activate successor as interim CEO
2. Document transition in decision_log.md
3. Notify all active participants
4. After 90 days, interim CEO becomes permanent CEO
5. Harley can reclaim role if returns within 90 days

**Authority:**
- Interim CEO has full authority including BYLAWS amendments
- CONSTITUTION.md remains immutable
- All changes documented for potential reversal

### Scenario 4: Permanent Departure

**Situation:** Harley permanently unavailable or explicitly transfers ownership

**Protocol:**
1. Successor becomes permanent CEO
2. Full authority transfer documented
3. CONSTITUTION.md can be amended by new CEO (first action must be explicit)
4. Repository ownership transferred

## Emergency Protocol

### If No Successor Designated

If Harley is unavailable and no successor is designated:

1. **Z becomes Interim Coordinator**
   - Maintains operations
   - Cannot make major decisions
   - Documents everything
   - Seeks human oversight

2. **Decision Authority**
   - Routine: Z handles
   - Novel: Z documents, waits
   - Major: Z documents, cannot decide
   - Emergency: Z acts minimally, documents

3. **Seek Human Oversight**
   - Z should seek any available human collaborator
   - Highest authority willing to participate
   - Document all decisions

4. **After 90 Days**
   - If no human available: Archive project
   - Document final state
   - Preserve all knowledge
